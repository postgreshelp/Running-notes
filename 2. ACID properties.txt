2. ACID

	Atomicity
	
		- irrespective of no.of statements, treat them as unique.
		
		begin;
		update emp set sal=sal-100;
		update emp set sal=sal+100;
		end;
		
	Consistency
	
	- Before & after your transaction, your database should be cons.
	
	Isolation
	
	- Reads/Writes should not interfere., Reads should see cons. past image
	- MVCC (may lead to lots of dead tuples)

	Durability
	
	- at anycost my data should be safe.
	- changes are captured in tx logs, as long as tx logs are present, pg can restore them.
